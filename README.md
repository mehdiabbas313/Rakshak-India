# Rakshak India

Rakshak India is an AI-powered emergency response and public safety platform designed to connect citizens with police stations, hospitals, emergency services, legal assistance, and trusted contacts.

> Project Status: Under Active Development

## Overview

During emergency situations, people often face difficulty contacting the correct authorities, sharing their live location, finding nearby services, or receiving timely legal assistance.

Rakshak India provides a unified platform for emergency response, public safety, women’s safety, police assistance, hospital discovery, FIR support, and legal help.

## Key Features

- Emergency SOS assistance
- Live location sharing
- Nearby police station discovery
- Nearby hospital discovery
- Women safety support
- Online FIR guidance and submission
- Legal assistance request system
- Emergency legal helpline
- Authentication and protected routes
- Admin and authority dashboards
- Dark and light theme support

## Technology Stack
